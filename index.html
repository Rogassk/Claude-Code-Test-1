FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out to our support team.

What is TaskFlow AI and how does it work?

TaskFlow AI is an intelligent productivity platform that uses artificial intelligence to automate task management, prioritization, and workflow optimization. It connects to your existing tools, analyzes your team's work patterns, and provides smart recommendations to help you deliver projects faster.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! Our Starter plan is completely free for up to 5 team members with no time limit. For the Pro plan, we offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.

Can I import my projects from other tools?

TaskFlow AI integrates with over 200 tools including Jira, Asana, Trello, Monday.com, Notion, Slack, GitHub, and more. You can import your existing projects with a single click, and all your data, history, and attachments come along.

How secure is my data?

Security is our top priority. We use AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. We're SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant, and offer SSO with SAML 2.0 on Enterprise plans. Your data is hosted on AWS with automatic backups and geographic redundancy.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Absolutely. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time from your account settings. If you cancel, you'll retain access through the end of your current billing period.

What kind of support do you offer?

Starter plans include access to our community forum and knowledge base. Pro plans include priority email support with a 4-hour response time. Enterprise plans receive a dedicated account manager, phone support, and a private Slack channel with our team.
